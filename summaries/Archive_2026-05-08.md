### Classic Literature
The list includes timeless classics such as *To Kill a Mockingbird*, *The Great Gatsby*, *Pride and Prejudice*, and *Frankenstein*, which are known for exploring complex themes like morality, social class, and human nature.
### Children's Tales
There are also children's stories like *Ginger bread man*, *Heidi*, and *Rumpelstilskin*, which often feature moral lessons and fantastical elements.
### Contemporary Works
Other books like *The Kite Runner*, *The Alchemist*, and *Bird Box* deal with more contemporary issues, including war, spirituality, and survival.
### Dystopian and Young Adult Fiction
Additionally, there are dystopian and young adult novels like *1984*, *Jannat Kay Pattay*, and *Horrid Henry*, which frequently explore themes of rebellion, identity, and the human condition.
