🌿 Bibliophile Concierge — Architecture 🌿

A 24-app, 3-workflow library system in n8n · "Tangled" cartoon-garden theme

🔴 Trigger (start) 🟠 Fetch / lookup 🟤 AI brain (Groq) 🟢 Store / log 💬 Notify / post

📖 Member 1 — Acquisition & Multi-Lingual Librarian

Brings new books in, detects language, applies the cartoon "plant spirit" theme.
📝n8n FormUser submits book title (any language)
↓
📚Google Books + Open LibraryLook up author, ISBN, summary (parallel)
↓
🌐Google TranslateDetect language of title
↓
🤖Groq LLM (free)Genre + Plant Spirit + price estimate
↓
🎨Unsplash → CloudinaryCartoon cover image, hosted
↓
🌱Airtable + Google SheetsPrimary vault + backup row
↓
💬WhatsApp"Planting Confirmation" message

📚 Member 2 — Inventory & Loan Manager

Tracks who has which book, weather-based suggestions, overdue nudges.
📝n8n Loan FormIssue or Return a book
↓
🔎AirtableUpdate Status: Issued / Available
↓
📅Google Calendar + TodoistReturn-date reminder
↓
📒Google SheetsLoan log (backup)
⏰Daily 9 AM cron
↓
🌤️OpenWeatherMapCloudy = Poetry day, Sunny = Adventure...
↓
✈️Telegram"Today's suggestion from your Poetry shelf"
⏰Daily overdue check
↓
📨Telegram (friend) + Discord (you)Polite nudges

✨ Member 3 — Engagement & Semantic Search

Daily motivation, "search inside" books, social archiving.
🌅Daily 8 AM cron
↓
🤖Groq LLMQuote + unique code
↓
📧Gmail + Telegram"Water Your Mind" message

↓
📁Airtable Notes + Google Drive PDFsParallel search
↓

✈️Telegram reply

↓
🤖Groq LLMGenerate captions / summary / tags
↓
🌐Instagram · LinkedIn · GitHub · Pinterest · YouTube · SpotifyMulti-channel archive
↓
📦Dropbox + Microsoft ExcelBackup & report log
